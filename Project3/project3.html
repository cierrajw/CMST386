Responsive Design

What is responsive design and why is it important? Responsive design is what allows a website to adjust to different screen sizes. It is very important because there are so many different types of devices with different screen sizes that will have access to your website. In order to create a pleasing and consistent experience for your users, responsive design is crucial.

Google Analytics

Three types of Google Analytics reports include:

  • Browser Report:

    This gives you information about the client's profile; so, devices they use, and conversion by browser

  • Efficiency Analysis Report:

    This report helps to determine things like number of unique users, total sessions, conversion rate, number of new users

  • Site Speed Report:

    This report allows you to understand which pages on your website need attention due to long loading pages, pages with high bounce rates, and pages that need improved UX.
